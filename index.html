MY HEART BELONGS TO YOU
- Sid

/**
*A LETTER OF LOVE
*/
My Dearest Love,

You are the most beautiful part of my life, my safe haven, and my greatest joy.

// People see the stars, but I see my entire galaxy in you.

From the moment I met you, my heart knew a new rhythm, one it had never danced to before;

// Your smile, your kindness, your laugh.

You’ve made my world brighter, my heart fuller, and my life worth cherishing;

Every moment with you feels like a blessing;

Your presence turns the ordinary into extraordinary;

You’ve taught me the true meaning of love;

Through every challenge, your love has been my strength;

// Your voice is my favorite melody.

I will love you not only today or tomorrow;

But every day, in ways big and small;

You are my everything;

The reason I wake up with a smile, the thought I fall asleep with;

So here’s my promise:

No matter what life brings, I will always stand by your side, loving and cherishing you endlessly;

And now, if my words have touched your heart, touch the heart below and feel my warmth.

I love you!!!
